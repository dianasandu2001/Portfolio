Bugmageddon

Genre: Top-Down Twin Stick Shooter, Mech, Bugs, Single Player | Platforms: PC | Engine: Unity

Overview

Bugmageddon takes place roughly a hundred years in the future, humanity has fully adapted nuclear technology and despite all odds come to a somewhat unified understanding of one another. As nuclear reactors across the globe power houses for billions, the waste of those reactors attracts some creatures to the surface and creates a few problems we need to now take care of.

Gameplay Features

🤖 Mech Combat: Pilot powerful mechs equipped with heavy weaponry to eliminate swarms of radioactive bugs.
🐜 Varied Mission Types: Experience five distinct missions, including extermination, traversal objectives, area defense, land recapture, and a climactic boss fight.
🧭 Objective-Based Gameplay: Progress through missions by completing clear goals, from moving between key locations to securing contaminated zones.
💥 Escalating Threats: Enemy intensity increases as missions progress, pushing players to adapt their tactics and positioning.
