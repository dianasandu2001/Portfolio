BloodBloom

Genre: Horror, Exploration, Atmospheric, Open-World | Platforms: PC | Engine: Unity

Overview

BloodBloom is a open world horror game inspired by Resident Evil 2-4. The player plays a detective that is taking over the case of a missing detective in order to find him and catch those who kidnapped him. The game consits of a tutorial like scene where the game plot is set and a main area in which all of the action happens.

Development Details

This is my second Portfolio project my courses during my studies in second year at Metropolio, part of the Game Development major.

This project is a team effort with @Fadak initially co-creating the nerative and @William joining us later and helping us shape it into the game it is today. All the models were done by our lovely artists @Juho, @Kalle, @Serhii, and @Denys. The development took aproximately 4 months consisiting of roughly 2 months of brianstorming, narrative design, and coding fundamental functions & 2 months of making models, piecing together said modles and combining them more code being developed, as well as testing and debugging.

The Github Repository is private because @Fadak and I had much bigger plans for the game that ended up not to be manageable in such a short period of time and have plans to make in it's entierty as a sequal to this, as well as more updates coming to this game in the near future.
